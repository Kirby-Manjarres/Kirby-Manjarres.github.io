ShapeShips-AI (Geometry Wars Recreation)

A neon-styled 2D shooter inspired by Geometry Wars: Retro Evolved, built with Processing and ported to p5.js for web play. Featuring AI-driven custom enemies with dynamic behaviors (homing, shielding, orbiting, erratic, boomerang).

Instructions: Move with arrow keys, aim with the mouse, and shoot with left-click. Deploy black hole bombs with right-click. Survive and score as high as you can!
