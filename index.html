Crypto Forecast Bot

An AI-powered chatbot that predicts future prices for popular cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH) using 10 years of historical data from Yahoo Finance. It leverages Facebook Prophet for forecasting and Gradio for the interactive chatbot interface. Commands: Type 'Predict BTC for next 7 days', 'Refresh ETH', etc. to get forecasts and plots.
